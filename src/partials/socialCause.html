BLOOD DONATION CAMP

FACTS

The average adult has about 10 pints of blood in his body,whereas roughly 1 pint is given during a donation

The shelf-life of donated blood is 35 to 42 days,there is a constant need to replenish stocks in our blood banks

Blood cannot be manufactured anywhere or synthesized indirectly,it can only come from generous donors

2 or 3 lives can be saved from the blood cells, plasma and platelets obtained from just a single unit of blood

STATISTICS

More than 38,000 blood donations are needed every day

According to the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare,in 2015-16,blood collection witnessed a shortage of 11.5 lakh units

India faced a 10 per cent shortage in its estimated blood requirement in 2015-16
