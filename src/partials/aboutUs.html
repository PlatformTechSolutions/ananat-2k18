About Anant

This year we present ANANT Twenty Eighteen, the 8th annual techno-cultural festival of Panjab University SSG Regional Centre, Hoshiarpur. We at PUSSGRC put our heart and soul into making this college extravaganza a great success. Collaboration is the key to success and ANANT provides you the platform for it. Our students have an urge for excellence and ANANT helps you fulfil it. ANANT is growing each year since 2009 and is transforming into one of the biggest techno-cultural fesivals in the area.

So, live the romance, live the rain, live the gusto, live the legacy and live the life, just here with us at ANANT Twenty Eighteen
