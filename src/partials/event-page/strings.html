About Event

Life is all about playing. This instrumental music competition encourages the contestants to exhibit their musical abilities and regale a captive audience.

Let’s make a difference and make playing an instrument a joyous event. Let the Music take you away & let the music set you free during Instrument Playing Competition. Get ready because its ANANT 2k18 – WORLD OF FICTION

Rules

  • Each team or participant could play one or more instruments
  • Each team can have not more than 4 members
  • Each team will be given 4-6 minutes to showcase their performance
  • Participants must bring their own equipment
  • All forms of music are allowed
  • The decision of the judges will be final and un-questionable
  • Participants must carry their college id cards otherwise their participation would be cancelled

Marking criteria

  • Rhythm – 20 marks
  • Transition – 10 marks
  • Grace & Energy – 10 marks
  • Crowd Interaction and overall impact – 10 marks
