Mock Parliment

About Event

The mock parliament is a fictional parliament which will give a practical experience to our young generation regarding the law making process which is carried down in our union legislature that is parliament. It seeks participation from students across all disciplines.

Schedule

DAY : 3

TIME : 10:00

Venue:Moot Court

Entry Fees

₹100

Rules

  • Participation will be only on individual basis.
  • The participants will be allotted their portfolios/roles on the spot by way of chit system i.e.,
    • Members of Ruling party (including Prime Minister)
    • Members of Opposition party (including Leader of Opposition party)
    • Others
  • After presenting the bill, and rebuttals, every member will be given a minute to conclude their speech
  • Language: Hindi/English/Punjabi
  • The judgment shall be given on the following basis(skills)
    • Relevancy of points
    • Oratory skills
    • Arguments
    • Decorum
    • Research
  • The prizes will be awarded in the following categories:Best Orator and Best Parliamentarian
  • The judgment of the Speaker/Judge will be final.

Marking criteria

  • Content of the debate
  • Flow of thoughts
  • Oratory Skills
