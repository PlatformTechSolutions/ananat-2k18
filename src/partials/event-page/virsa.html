About Event

Dancing is nothing but dreaming with your feet!

Entry Fees

₹50 per person

Rules

  • Participants must carry their ID cards with them else their participation will be cancelled
  • All forms of folk dances (whether modern or traditional) are acceptable (Bhangra,Giddha, Natti, Luddi, Malwai , etc. )
  • Each team is allowed to perform between 8-12 minutes. In the event that a team exceeds the 12 minute time limit a 10 point deduction will be made from each judge’s evaluation form
  • Teams must be comprised of at least 8 dancers and no more than 16 dancers. A dholi and/or singer may also be on the team aside from the 16 dancers. Exceptions may be made at the discretion of the organizers
  • Time starts as soon as your music begins (including any instrumental or vocal introduction)
  • Any sort of traditional instruments can be used in the performance
  • No member shall be added or replaced to the team on the last moment
  • All team members must provide the appropriate identification when they check in (Photo id). Please note Photo Id includes–(College Identification Card,Adhaar card,Driving Licence)
  • Each team must bring a soft copy of the track in a Pendrive and DVD with their music. The soft copy should be submitted to the organisers 2 hours before the event
  • All teams must provide their team name and the name of the members during registrations
  • The decision given by the judges will be final and unquestionable

Marking criteria

  • Opening - 10 marks
  • Costumes - 10 marks
  • Synchronization, Fluidity, Formations, Transitions - 25 marks
  • Grace, Energy – 15 marks
  • Traditional Steps -10 marks
  • Face Expressions - 10 marks
  • Instruments Used - 5 marks
  • Ending – 10 marks
