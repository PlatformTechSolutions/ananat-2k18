Rules

  • Participants are allowed to use only English. Use of any other language will lead to negative marking
  • The time limit is 3 minutes
  • Participants can be asked to speak either FOR or AGAINST the topic which would be decided on the basis of draw of lots
  • Individual participation (no teams)
  • Use of sheets/paper or any other reading material or electronic gadget is not allowed
  • Use of aggression and abusive language is strictly not allowed
  • Decision of the judge is final and abiding

Marking criteria

  • Content of the debate
  • Flow of thoughts
  • Oratory Skills
