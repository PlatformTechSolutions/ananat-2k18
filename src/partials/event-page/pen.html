Pen your fiction

About Event

A writing competition brought to you by Anant 2018. Open to all !

Rules

  • There are two categories : English and Hindi.
  • Only one entry per person will be accepted in each category.
  • The entries shouldn't exceed four lines of work and must be original.
  • Only single author entries qualify (no co-authored entries)
  • Entrants must like and share Anant's fb page on their wall.
  • Abusive or racist content won't be entertained.
  • Each entry must comprise of the written work onto a self-selected background without patent/name of the author.
  • The background comprises of 5% of the total score.
  • Entries which incorporate known fictional characters will be awarded extra points.
  • Entries must be submitted by midnight Feburary 22, 2018.
  • Mail entries to: Entries@anantfest.in With the subject : Pen your fiction.
  • The entries will be posted anonymously on Anant's Facebook page.
  • The judgment shall be given on the following basis(skills)
    • Best English fiction
    • Best Hindi fiction
    • People's choice
  • The "Best English and Hindi fiction" will be judged by a panel and "People's choice" would be awarded primarily on the maximum number of likes.
  • Winners will receive immense bragging rights along with certificates and Anant goodies.
  • Any failure to adhere to these contest rules may result in disqualification.
  • THE PEN YOUR FICTION TEAM is not responsible for late, lost, stolen, misdirected, incomplete or illegible entries.
